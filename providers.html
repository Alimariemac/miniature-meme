Why Queerly Health?

56% of LGBTQ+ people experience discrimination in healthcare settings. Including, like 29% of trans people, being denied care by healthcare providers.

Barriers  to access, like discrimination, and gaps in access lead to LGBTQ+ specific health disparities. Our community experiences greater risk for contracting STDs, cancer, cardiovascular disease, diabetes, mental illness, dementia, and more.

Why Digital Health?

Digital Health, like Telehealth and Mobile Health, has the ability to work around barriers to accessing healthcare. Digital Health increases access, produces high user satisfaction, and facilitates better patient/client outcomes. All while delivering the same quality of care, in less time.

How Queerly Health works:

1. Create your Provider Profile

Let the individuals on our marketplace know who you are as a provider.

2. Vetting and Training

We’ll get to know you as a provider first and then you can choose your membership,

3. Schedule your Availability

Choose when you are available in-person and/or via telehealth whenever is convenient for you.

4. Accept new Appointments

Use our platform to accept in-person and/or telehealth appointments.

5. Meet with Individuals

Conduct your appointments with LGBTQ+ individuals in-person and/or  via telehealth.

6. Treatment Plan and Follow-Up Care

Offer LGBTQ+ individuals a treatment plan, schedule labs, prescriptions, and referrals.

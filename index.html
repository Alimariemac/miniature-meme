Vetting and Training

Health and wellness professionals, or providers, go through a screening process and sometimes additional training before joining Queerly Health. We do this to ensure providers are safe and knowledgeable enough to work with LGBTQ+ persons.

Digital Health

Individuals may search Queerly Health’s online marketplace for providers that are right for them and then book appointments in-clinic or through telehealth, like a video visit.
